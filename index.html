Cool Sculpting

During a CoolSculpting treatment session, a specialized device is used to apply controlled cooling to the targeted area of the body, such as the abdomen, flanks, thighs, or arms. The cooling technology freezes and crystallizes the fat cells without harming the surrounding skin, nerves, or tissues. Over time, the frozen fat cells undergo apoptosis (natural cell death) and are gradually eliminated from the body through the natural metabolic processes.
